Aloft Dallas

Aloft Dallas offers an innovative stay experience with its urban-inspired design and modern amenities. Located in the heart of downtown Dallas, this hotel is perfect for both business and leisure travelers. The spacious loft-like rooms come equipped with ultra-comfortable beds, 42-inch flat-screen TVs, and fast & free WiFi. The hotel’s Re:mix lounge and W XYZ® bar are great spots for socializing and enjoying signature cocktails. For fitness enthusiasts, the Re:charge gym is open 24/7, and the Splash pool provides a refreshing escape from the Texas heat.

One of the standout features of Aloft Dallas is its pet-friendly policy, ensuring that your furry friends are welcome during your stay. Additionally, the hotel offers convenient access to popular attractions such as the Dallas Convention Center, the Dallas World Aquarium, and the Sixth Floor Museum at Dealey Plaza. Guests can also take advantage of the hotel’s complimentary shuttle service within a 3-mile radius. With its vibrant atmosphere, excellent location, and top-notch services, Aloft Dallas is an ideal choice for those seeking a unique and memorable stay in the city.

The Re:mix lounge serves as a central gathering spot for guests to relax, work, or socialize. The space features contemporary furniture, large windows that let in plenty of natural light, and a pool table for some friendly competition. Adjacent to the lounge is the W XYZ® bar, where guests can enjoy a variety of handcrafted cocktails, local beers, and small bites. The bar often hosts live music events and DJ performances, adding to the lively ambiance of the hotel.

Aloft Dallas also caters to business travelers with its fully equipped meeting rooms and business center. The Tactic meeting space offers state-of-the-art audiovisual equipment, high-speed internet access, and flexible seating arrangements to accommodate different types of events. Whether you’re planning a corporate meeting, a seminar, or a small conference, the hotel staff is dedicated to ensuring your event runs smoothly.

For those who value wellness and fitness, the Re:charge gym at Aloft Dallas is a major highlight. The fitness center is open 24/7 and features a wide range of equipment, including cardio machines, free weights, and strength training machines. Guests can also take advantage of the hotel's outdoor Splash pool, which is perfect for a refreshing dip or lounging by the water. The pool area is equipped with comfortable seating and umbrellas, providing a relaxing oasis in the middle of the city.

In terms of dining options, Aloft Dallas offers the Re:fuel by Aloft℠ pantry, a self-serve gourmet eatery open 24/7. Guests can grab a quick snack, a light meal, or a beverage anytime, day or night. The pantry features a variety of healthy choices, including salads, sandwiches, yogurt, and fresh fruit, as well as indulgent treats like pastries and chips. For a more substantial meal, the hotel's location in downtown Dallas provides easy access to numerous restaurants, cafes, and bars, offering a wide range of culinary delights.

The hotel's commitment to sustainability and eco-friendly practices is another reason to choose Aloft Dallas for your stay. The property implements various green initiatives, such as energy-efficient lighting, water-saving fixtures, and recycling programs. Guests can also participate in the Make a Green Choice program, which rewards them for opting out of housekeeping services, helping to conserve resources and reduce their environmental impact.
