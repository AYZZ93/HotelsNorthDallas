Candlewood Suites Dallas

Candlewood Suites Dallas offers a home-like atmosphere for extended stays, making it an excellent choice for business travelers, families, and those relocating to the area. The spacious suites are designed with comfort and convenience in mind, featuring fully equipped kitchens, comfortable living areas, and high-speed internet access. The hotel’s Candlewood Cupboard® provides a variety of snacks, beverages, and essentials, available 24/7.

Guests can take advantage of the hotel’s complimentary laundry facilities and outdoor grilling area, perfect for enjoying a meal with family or colleagues. The hotel also offers a 24-hour fitness center, ensuring guests can maintain their workout routines during their stay. Candlewood Suites Dallas is pet-friendly, welcoming furry friends to join guests in their suites.

The hotel’s location provides easy access to major highways, making it convenient for exploring Dallas and the surrounding areas. Nearby attractions include the Galleria Dallas, NorthPark Center, and the George W. Bush Presidential Library and Museum. The friendly and accommodating staff at Candlewood Suites Dallas are dedicated to providing exceptional service and ensuring a comfortable and enjoyable stay for all guests.
